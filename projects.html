lifesci-methodology

Personal methodology library for life sciences investing. Skills, frameworks, and reference data for biopharma drug valuation, equity research, and pre-IPO pipeline tracking.

A consolidated repository of analytical frameworks, reusable Claude Code skills, and curated reference data accumulated across drug models and equity coverage. Includes 7 Claude Code skills (drug model, clinical landscape, A1 pipeline update, BD tracker, Howard Marks investment framework, and more), 4 methodology references (bottom-up 6-step revenue model, SOTP DCF cheat-sheet, Howard Marks philosophy, royalty monetization waterfall), and curated datasets covering 80+ indications with epidemiology, pricing, and market share parameters. Designed to be auto-discoverable by Claude Code via symlinks.

biotech-bd-tracker

Cross-border out-licensing deal tracker for Greater China, Japan, and Korea biotech. A sourcing engine for structured capital opportunities.

Systematic tracker of ~175 biopharma out-licensing transactions (2020–2026) across GC/JP/KR licensors, organized into Actionable (35 deals at Ph2/3+ ex-China) and Pipeline (140 deals at earlier stages) sheets. 15-column schema captures licensor, licensee, asset/target/MoA, modality, development stage by region (US/CN/EU/JP), deal value, rights structure, and analyst notes. Modality coverage spans small molecules, ADCs, monoclonal antibodies, siRNA, peptides, cell therapy, and fusion proteins.
