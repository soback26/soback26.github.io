Personal methodology library for life sciences investing. Skills, frameworks, and reference data purpose-built for private equity deal execution and biotech innovative drug pipeline analysis and valuation.

A consolidated, private repository of analytical frameworks, reusable Claude Code skills, and curated reference data accumulated across biotech pipeline diligence and private equity deal execution. biotech-bd-tracker

Cross-border out-licensing deal tracker for Greater China, Japan, and Korea biotech. A sourcing engine for structured capital opportunities.

Systematic tracker of ~175 biopharma out-licensing transactions (2020–2026) across GC/JP/KR licensors, organized into Actionable (35 deals at Ph2/3+ ex-China) and Pipeline (140 deals at earlier stages) sheets. 15-column schema captures licensor, licensee, asset/target/MoA, modality, development stage by region (US/CN/EU/JP), deal value, rights structure, and analyst notes. Modality coverage spans small molecules, ADCs, monoclonal antibodies, siRNA, peptides, cell therapy, and fusion proteins.

hkex-a1-pipeline

A web-scraping and structured-data tool for financial analysts tracking new Application Proof (A1) filings on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange.

Automated monthly workflow that ingests HKEXnews A1 filing listings, fetches each filer's application proof, and populates a 14-column tracker covering corporate structure, sector, listing chapter (18A biotech / 18C specialist tech / main board), sponsors, headline financials, lead asset, and analyst highlights. Designed for financial analysts who need a systematic, refreshable view of pre-IPO candidates without manually cross-referencing PDFs. Covers ~150 life-science filers across 2024–2026 and handles re-filings when applications lapse after the six-month HKEX window.
